Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 15, 2026

wedding-tv.cn (hereafter "the Website") respects and protects user privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information.

1. Types of Information Collected

a) Information You Actively Provide

  • Contact Form Data: When you submit a domain offer or inquiry, we collect your name, email, phone/WeChat, offer amount, and message.
  • E-Invitation Tool Data: When using our e-invitation generator, optional inputs (couple names, wedding date) are stored locally in your browser only, not uploaded to our servers.

b) Automatically Collected Information

  • Access Logs: IP address, browser type, access time, requested pages, and referrer pages—used for service improvement and security.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies:
    • Google Analytics: Tracks traffic sources and user behavior to help us understand site usage.
    • Google AdSense: Used for ad delivery and attribution; Google displays relevant ads based on your interests.
    • Essential Cookies: Language preference, session information, etc.
  • Approximate Geolocation: Derived from IP address; not precise location tracking.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Process your domain offer and inquiry, communicating transfer procedures and terms.
  • Improve website features, content, and user experience.
  • Prevent fraud, abuse, and security threats.
  • Recommend relevant tools and resources.
  • Send necessary service notices and updates.
  • Analyze website performance and user behavior (aggregated and de-identified).

3. Data Sharing with Third Parties

  • Google Analytics and AdSense: We use Google services for traffic analysis and ad delivery. Google processes your data according to its Privacy Policy.
  • Transfer Platforms: If you transfer via Aliyun, Ename, or Escrow, your information is shared with those platforms to complete the transaction.
  • Email Service: We communicate via email; your email address is used for this purpose.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law or government request.

4. Data Retention

  • Contact Form Data: Retained in email; you may request deletion anytime. Server access logs typically retained for 90 days.
  • Cookies: Most browser cookies expire when the session ends or you clear browsing data; persistent cookies typically expire after 1–2 years.
  • Google Analytics Data: Retained for 14 months by default.

Under applicable data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Know what personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct: Request updates or corrections to inaccurate information.
  • Delete: Request deletion of your personal data (in certain cases).
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for cookies and tracking.
  • Lodge a Complaint: File a complaint with relevant data protection authorities.
